Privacy Policy

This policy covers the Tether website only. Here's exactly what this site does — and doesn't — collect.

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The short version

This website does not collect any personal information, set cookies, or run analytics. The only external service it contacts is Google Fonts. That's it.

Who we are

Tether is a private memory vault app for iOS. This policy covers the Tether marketing website only — not the app. The app has its own separate privacy policy.

If you have questions, reach us at lukaschristnyc@gmail.com.

What this website collects

Nothing, directly. This site has no forms, no sign-ups, no analytics scripts, and no tracking cookies. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information when you visit.

The only external service this site contacts is Google Fonts, which serves the typefaces used on the page. When your browser loads a font, it makes a request to Google's servers — and as part of that standard HTTP request, Google receives your IP address. We have no control over how Google processes this. You can read Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Cookies

This site sets no cookies of any kind — not for analytics, not for preferences, not for tracking.

Third-party links

This site links to the Apple App Store. Clicking that link takes you to Apple's platform, which has its own privacy practices. We have no visibility into what Apple collects when you visit or download from the App Store.

Changes to this policy

If we add new features to this website that affect privacy — such as a contact form or analytics — we will update this page and the date below before doing so.

Questions?

If anything here is unclear, get in touch. We respond personally.
